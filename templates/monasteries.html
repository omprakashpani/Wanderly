Monasteries

Rumtek Dharma Chakra Centre

Rumtek, Gangtok, East Sikkim

The Rumtek Dharma Chakra Centre in Sikkim is a major Buddhist monastery, known for its sacred relics, vibrant murals, and as a key seat of the Kagyu tradition.

Pemayangtse Monastery

Pelling, West Sikkim

Pemayangtse Monastery, near Pelling in Sikkim, is one of the oldest Nyingma monasteries, known for its serene setting, ancient art, and stunning Kanchenjunga views.

Phodong Monastery

Phodong, North Sikkim

Phodong Monastery, located in North Sikkim, is a major Buddhist monastery of the Karma Kagyu sect. Built in the 18th century, it is famous for its vibrant murals, peaceful surroundings, and annual Cham masked dances.

Phensang Monastery

Phensang, North Sikkim

Phensang Monastery, in North Sikkim, belongs to the Nyingma sect and is known for its scenic location, colorful festivals, and annual Cham dances that draw many visitors.

Kartok Monastery

Kartok Lake, Yuksom, West Sikkim

Kartok Monastery, situated near Yuksom in West Sikkim, is a small yet beautiful monastery of the Nyingma sect. Surrounded by tranquil landscapes and a serene lake, it is known for its peaceful ambiance and spiritual charm.

Gonjang Monastery

Tashi Viewpoint, Gangtok, East Sikkim

Gonjang Monastery, near Gangtok in Sikkim, was established in 1981 and follows the Nyingma tradition. It is admired for its vibrant architecture, Buddhist teachings, and the serene atmosphere it offers to visitors.
