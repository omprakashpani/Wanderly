Featured Monasteries

Rumtek Dharma Chakra Centre

Rumtek, Gangtok, East Sikkim

The Rumtek Dharma Chakra Centre in Sikkim is a major Buddhist monastery, known for its sacred relics, vibrant murals, and as a key seat of the Kagyu tradition.

Pemayangtse Monastery

Pelling, West Sikkim

Pemayangtse Monastery, near Pelling in Sikkim, is one of the oldest Nyingma monasteries, known for its serene setting, ancient art, and stunning Kanchenjunga views.

Phodong Monastery

Phodong, North Sikkim

Phodong Monastery, located in North Sikkim, is a major Buddhist monastery of the Karma Kagyu sect. Built in the 18th century, it is famous for its vibrant murals, peaceful surroundings, and annual Cham masked dances.
